Mental Health Trends Among Students (June–November)

Data Visualization Project by Isha Parihariya

About This Project

This visualization displays four key indicators – Stress, Sleep, Mood, and Productivity – across six months. The goal is to understand how academic load, exams, and holidays affect students' mental health patterns.

Data Workflow

The data was entered in Google Sheets, cleaned for consistency, exported as CSV, and then visualized using Tableau Public. This ensures a clean and accurate dataset for generating insights.

Key Insights

  • Stress is highest during exam months (June, October).
  • Sleep and mood improve considerably during holidays (July).
  • Productivity is strongly influenced by stress and rest levels.
